Social brainstorming for improving our city!

Ideas-ba aims to be a social website that allows citizens to share ideas, complaints, questions and proposal to improve our cities. It is inspired in sites like StackOverflow, that allows collective knowledge to help us solve the problems that as developers we face everyday.
